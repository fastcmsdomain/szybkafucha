Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Szybka Fucha?

Szybka Fucha is a mobile app that connects people who need help with small tasks to verified local contractors. You can delegate shopping, cleaning, small repairs, pet care and much more.

How can I earn as a contractor?

After registering as a contractor, you'll complete a short identity verification. Then you can browse available tasks in your area, accept the ones that suit you and set your own rates. You'll receive payouts quickly and securely.

Is using the app safe?

Safety is our priority. All contractors undergo identity verification (KYC). Payments are secured by Stripe. You can also rate after each task, and our support team is available 24/7.
