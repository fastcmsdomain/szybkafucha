# Product Positioning

## What Szybka Fucha Is

Szybka Fucha is a mobile marketplace for immediate small local tasks. The product connects people who need help with nearby helpers who want flexible earning opportunities.

Core value proposition from the repo:
- Busy person problem: "I don't have time to pick up my package / do shopping / wait in line"
- Product promise: someone nearby can handle it quickly
- Differentiators mentioned in repo materials: speed, local matching, GPS tracking, in-app payments, flexible earning, fast communication

## Current Go-To-Market Reality

- This is still a pre-launch product with newsletter and waitlist capture.
- The landing page acts as both lead capture and market research.
- The current site asks for:
  - name
  - email
  - city
  - user type: `client` or `contractor`
  - marketing consent
- The landing copy already emphasizes co-creation: "Twoja opinia tworzy aplikację".

Do not write copy that sounds like the nationwide product is already fully live unless the user explicitly wants speculative concept copy.

## Core Audiences

### Client

Profile:
- Busy professional, typically urban
- Wants to delegate small errands and household tasks
- Values speed, convenience, and trust

Pain points:
- No time for shopping, queues, repairs, cleaning, errands
- Friction in finding reliable local help fast
- Small jobs are too minor for traditional service providers

Messages that fit:
- odzyskaj czas
- znajdź pomoc w okolicy
- zleć drobne zadania szybko
- nie trać dnia na małe sprawy

### Contractor

Profile:
- Student, gig worker, part-time earner, entrepreneurial local helper
- Wants flexible income and simple access to nearby jobs
- Values control over schedule and transparent earning

Pain points:
- Hard to find reliable, repeatable gig work
- Existing options feel slow, inconsistent, or disconnected from local demand

Messages that fit:
- zarabiaj na swoich zasadach
- wybieraj zlecenia blisko siebie
- dorób wtedy, kiedy chcesz
- buduj reputację i zdobywaj kolejne zlecenia

## Proven or Repo-Backed Message Anchors

Use these because they already exist in the codebase or project docs:
- "Zleć lub Zarabiaj. Lokalnie. Natychmiast."
- "Znajdź ją w 5 minut."
- Nearby help for:
  - zakupy i dostawy
  - sprzątanie i porządki
  - drobne naprawy
  - opieka nad zwierzętami
- Contractor-side hooks:
  - elastyczne godziny pracy
  - zarabiaj nawet 150 zł/h
  - szybkie wypłaty
  - buduj swoją reputację
- Co-creation hooks:
  - współtwórz funkcje aplikacji
  - wpływaj na rozwój platformy
  - priorytetowy start w Twoim mieście

## Claims Discipline

Safe claims:
- pre-launch waitlist
- app in development
- local task marketplace
- built for small everyday tasks
- early access, city priority, co-creation

Use caution with:
- exact launch timing
- city rollout dates beyond "Warsaw first" unless the user specifies newer plans
- hard performance promises
- absolute trust or safety claims
- guaranteed earnings

Avoid unless the user provides confirmation:
- "available now across Poland"
- "thousands of users already joined"
- "guaranteed jobs"
- "best in Poland"
- "verified helpers everywhere"

## Tone

Preferred tone:
- practical
- energetic
- direct
- human
- urban and local

Avoid:
- empty hype
- corporate phrases
- generic startup language
- long abstract intros before the CTA

## Default CTA Bank

Use or adapt these:
- Zapisz się na listę oczekujących
- Dołącz do pierwszych użytkowników
- Zarezerwuj miejsce w swoim mieście
- Pomóż nam stworzyć aplikację idealną dla Ciebie
- Dołącz i otrzymuj informacje o starcie
- Sprawdź, kiedy ruszamy w Twoim mieście
